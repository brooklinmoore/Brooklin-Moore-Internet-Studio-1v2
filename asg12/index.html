Favorite Movies Gallery

These movies inspire me for their creativity, storytelling, and emotion. Each one has something unique — from adventure and mystery to music, humor, and deep meaning.

Memento

A psychological thriller about memory, identity, and the way we piece together our own truths.

The Princess and the Frog

A heartfelt story about dreams, love, and determination set in the lively world of New Orleans.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

The magical beginning of a legendary story about friendship, courage, and discovering one’s destiny.

Monster High: Boo York

A colorful musical adventure where ghouls chase their dreams in the big city under a magical comet.

Fight Club

A dark and thought-provoking film about identity, rebellion, and the search for meaning in chaos.

Interstellar

An epic journey through space and time, exploring love, sacrifice, and humanity’s survival.

The Prince of Egypt

A visually stunning animated retelling of faith, freedom, and brotherhood drawn from ancient history.

Knives Out

A clever modern mystery filled with twists, wit, and an unforgettable detective story.

Rio

A fun and colorful journey about courage, friendship, and finding your wings in unexpected ways.

The Greatest Showman

A vibrant musical celebrating imagination, diversity, and the beauty of chasing your dreams.

Uncle Drew

A hilarious, heartwarming basketball comedy about teamwork, redemption, and believing in yourself.

Mulan

A story of bravery and honor as a young woman defies expectations to protect her family and country.
