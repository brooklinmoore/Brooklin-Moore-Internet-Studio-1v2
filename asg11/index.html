The Lightning Bolts

Meet The Lightning Bolts — a dodgeball team built for speed, skill, and style. Each player brings a unique edge to dominate any match. From quick reflexes to unstoppable power, this crew never misses a shot.

Goku

With unmatched energy and determination, Goku’s agility and quick reflexes make him an unstoppable force in dodgeball battles.

Elsa

Elsa’s icy precision and cool composure keep the team calm under pressure, and her focus ensures every throw is on target.

Naruto Uzumaki

Energetic and fearless, Naruto’s unpredictable moves and shadow clone strategy make him the ultimate offensive player.

Buzz Lightyear

Armed with bravery and a strong arm, Buzz brings team spirit and precision throws “to infinity and beyond.”

Mikasa Ackerman

Strong, strategic, and fearless, Mikasa’s defense and lightning-fast reaction time keep the team unbeatable.
