Places To Eat

The Three Cauldrons

Come dine at The Three Cauldrons! A lively little tavern where guests can enjoy rich British favorites like shepherd’s pie bubbling in miniature cauldrons, golden pasties, hearty stews, and fish n chips. The star of the show, however, is their world-famous butter beer, enjoyed by all the students at Hogwarts and other non-sorcerers alike. They’ve got music always playing, starting in the daytime and going all through the night. Here you can eat, drink, dance, and have a great time.

Honeydukes Express

If you or your travel partners have a sweet tooth, step into Honeydukes Express. A cozy candy and dessert shop that specializes in everything sweet. Try Wizarding World favorites like Bertie Bott's Every Flavor Beans, Chocolate Frogs, and Pumpkin Pasties, Cauldron Cakes, and Licorice Wands.There's so many rare flavors that literally come to life. Visitors can watch candies being crafted, sample new magical confections, and take home boxes of colorful delights straight from the heart of Honeydukes.

Hogwarts Great Hall Feast

If you’d like to stay in Hogwarts, they host the Hogwarts Great Hall Feast every night for their guests. An array of foods from honey glazed chicken to garlic roasted corn, they have all the comfort food a person could ask for. The hall is far more grand than any favorite restaurant and far tastier. With a homemade charm, the Grand Hall Feast has something even the pickiest of eaters are sure to enjoy. Every meal is served under the enchanted ceiling, where floating candles and a beautiful evening sky make each dining experience a truly magical experience.
