Things To Do

Hogwarts Castle Tour

Explore every corner of Hogwarts Castle, from the moving staircases to the enchanted Great Hall. Learn spells and join interactive classes in potion-making, charms, and magical history.

Quidditch Experience

Take to the skies on a broomstick and practice Quidditch. Watch professional matches and even play friendly games with other visitors.

Magical Creatures Encounter

Visit magical forests and creature sanctuaries to meet hippogriffs, dragons, and more. Learn how to care for and interact safely with magical beasts.
