What is the difference between structured content and semantic HTML?

The difference between structured content and semantic HTML is the way the content is delivered and the purpose of it. Sturcted content is delivered in chunks and focus on organizing information in a way thats reusable and defined. Semantic HTML however, is Web-spcific, so all of the tags already have meaning. Structured content is made for machines, while semantic HTML is made for the users.
